Jolly Ranchers are one of the most beloved and iconic candy brands in the world, and for good reason! Jolly Ranchers come in a variety of delicious and mouth-watering flavors, such as watermelon, green apple, cherry, grape, blue raspberry, and many more. Jolly Ranchers were originally made as a hard candy for children to enjoy. They were created in 1949 by Bill Harmsen and Don Welch, who were looking for a new candy to sell in their Denver, Colorado ice cream parlor.

The name "Jolly Rancher" was inspired by the Jolly Roger, which is the name of the skull and crossbones flag flown by pirates.

Jolly Ranchers were actually used as a form of currency in some prisons in the United States. Inmates would trade them for other goods or services.

Jolly Ranchers have been featured in a number of movies and TV shows, including the 1995 movie "Clueless" and the TV show "Breaking Bad."

Jolly Ranchers are not only enjoyed as a candy, but they are also used as a flavoring for a variety of other products, including vodka, soda, and lip balm.
